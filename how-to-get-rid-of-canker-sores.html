Home remedies

Home remedies are a popular way to treat canker sores. They are often less expensive and more convenient than over-the-counter medications, and they can be just as effective. There are a number of different home remedies that can be used to treat canker sores, including:

  • Salt water rinse

    Rinsing the mouth with salt water can help to clean the canker sore and reduce pain. To make a salt water rinse, dissolve 1/2 teaspoon of salt in 8 ounces of warm water. Rinse the mouth for 30 seconds, then spit out the water. Repeat several times a day.


  • Baking soda paste

    Baking soda can help to neutralize the acid in the canker sore and reduce pain. To make a baking soda paste, mix 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda with a little water to form a paste. Apply the paste to the canker sore and leave it on for several minutes. Rinse the mouth with water.


  • Yogurt or other fermented foods

    Yogurt and other fermented foods contain probiotics, which are beneficial bacteria that can help to fight the bacteria that cause canker sores. Eating yogurt or other fermented foods may help to reduce the pain and discomfort of canker sores and speed up healing.


  • Honey

    Honey has antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties that can help to treat canker sores. Apply a small amount of honey to the canker sore and leave it on for several minutes. Rinse the mouth with water.
