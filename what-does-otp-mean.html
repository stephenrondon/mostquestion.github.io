A Beginner's Guide to OTP: Understanding the Basics


OTP stands for One True Pairing. It is a term used in fandom to refer to the pairing of two characters that the fan believes is the only true and perfect pairing for each other. OTPs are often based on the characters' chemistry, their shared interests, and their compatibility. Fans of OTPs often create fan fiction, fan art, and other creative works that explore the relationship between the two characters.

OTPs can be important to fans for a number of reasons. They can provide a sense of community and belonging, as fans of the same OTP can connect with each other and share their love for the pairing. Additionally, OTPs can help fans to explore their own sexuality and identity, as they can see themselves reflected in the characters they ship.
