A Masterclass: Putting On A Condom With Ease and Confidence

A condom is a thin sheath made of latex, polyurethane, or another material that is worn over the penis during sex. Condoms are used to prevent pregnancy and protect against sexually transmitted infections (STIs). Condoms are available in a variety of sizes, shapes, and colors.

Condoms are an effective way to prevent pregnancy and STIs. They are easy to use and can be purchased at most drugstores and convenience stores. Condoms have been used for centuries, and they have played a significant role in reducing the incidence of unintended pregnancy and STIs.

To put on a condom, follow these steps:

  1. Open the condom package and remove the condom.
  2. Place the condom on the tip of the erect penis.
  3. Roll the condom down the penis until it covers the entire shaft.
  4. Leave a small space at the tip of the condom to collect semen.
After sex, remove the condom and throw it away. Do not reuse condoms.
