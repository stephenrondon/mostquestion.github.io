When Does Costco Close? The Definitive Guide

Costco Wholesale Corporation, commonly known as Costco, is an American multinational corporation that operates a chain of membership-only warehouse clubs. Costco is known for its bulk discounts on a wide variety of products, including groceries, electronics, and home goods. The company has over 800 locations worldwide, including the United States, Canada, Mexico, the United Kingdom, and Australia.

Costco stores typically operate from 10:00 AM to 8:30 PM, Monday through Friday. On weekends, Costco stores are open from 9:30 AM to 6:00 PM. However, it is important to note that Costco store hours may vary by location. For example, some Costco stores in major cities may have extended hours, while Costco stores in rural areas may have shorter hours. It is always best to check with your local Costco store to verify their hours of operation.

Costco's hours of operation are designed to accommodate the needs of its members. The company's weekday hours are convenient for people who work traditional 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM jobs. The company's weekend hours are convenient for people who have more free time on the weekends. Costco also offers extended hours on some holidays, such as Thanksgiving and Christmas.
