The Ultimate Guide to Converting Millimeters to Centimeters

The metric system is a system of measurement that is based on the decimal system. The basic unit of length in the metric system is the meter. A centimeter (cm) is one hundredth of a meter, while a millimeter (mm) is one thousandth of a meter. Therefore, there are 10 millimeters in one centimeter.

The metric system is used in many countries around the world, including most of Europe, Asia, and South America. It is also the official system of measurement in science and engineering.
