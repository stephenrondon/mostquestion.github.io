A Quick Guide To: How To Change Your Name On Facebook?

Changing your name on Facebook is a simple process that can be completed in a few steps. It is important to note that you can only change your name once every 60 days, so it is important to choose a name that you are happy with. You can change your name to your real name, a nickname, or even a business name.

To change your name on Facebook, follow these steps:

  1. Click on the down arrow in the top right corner of the Facebook homepage.
  2. Select "Settings & Privacy" from the drop-down menu.
  3. Click on "Settings" in the left-hand menu.
  4. Click on the "Name" tab.
  5. Enter your new name in the "Name" field.
  6. Click on the "Review Change" button.
  7. Enter your Facebook password and click on the "Save Changes" button.

Your name will now be changed on Facebook. It may take a few minutes for the change to take effect.
