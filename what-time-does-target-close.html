The Ultimate Guide: What Time Does Target Close?

Target Corporation is the eighth-largest retailer in the United States, and the second-largest discount store retailer, behind Walmart. It was founded in 1902 and is headquartered in Minneapolis, Minnesota. The name Target comes from the bullseye logo, which the company has used since 1962. Target stores typically operate from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM, but hours may vary by location.

Target is a popular shopping destination for many reasons. It offers a wide variety of products, including groceries, clothing, home goods, and electronics. Target is also known for its competitive prices and its frequent sales and promotions.

If you're planning a trip to Target, be sure to check the store hours for your location. You can also find more information about Target on the company's website.
