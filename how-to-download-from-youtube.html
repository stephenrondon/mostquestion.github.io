  • Creative Commons Licenses: Some YouTube videos are licensed under Creative Commons licenses, which allow for downloading and reuse under certain conditions, such as attribution or non-commercial use.

By understanding the legal implications and respecting copyright laws, users can avoid potential legal issues and contribute to a fair and ethical digital ecosystem.
