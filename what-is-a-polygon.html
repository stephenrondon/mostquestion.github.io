Classification

The classification of polygons is an important part of understanding their properties and applications. Polygons can be classified into different types based on their number of sides, their angles, and their symmetry.

  • Number of sides: Polygons can be classified based on the number of sides they have. Common types of polygons include triangles (3 sides), quadrilaterals (4 sides), pentagons (5 sides), hexagons (6 sides), and so on.
  • Angles: Polygons can also be classified based on the measure of their angles. Polygons with all right angles are called rectangles, polygons with all equal angles are called regular polygons, and polygons with no equal angles are called irregular polygons.
  • Symmetry: Polygons can also be classified based on their symmetry. Polygons with rotational symmetry are called regular polygons, and polygons with reflection symmetry are called centrally symmetric polygons.

The classification of polygons helps us to understand their properties and applications.
