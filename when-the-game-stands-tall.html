When The Game Stands Tall? is a 2014 American sports drama film directed by Thomas Carter and written by Scott Marshall Smith and David Zelon, based on the 2003 non-fiction book of the same name by Neil Hayes. The film follows Bob Ladouceur, the head football coach of De La Salle High School in Concord, California, who led the school to a national record 151-game winning streak from 1992 to 2003.
