Dietary changes

Dietary changes can play an important role in preventing and treating hemorrhoids. Eating a healthy diet that is high in fiber and low in fat can help to keep bowel movements soft and regular, which can reduce the risk of developing hemorrhoids. Fiber helps to bulk up stool and make it easier to pass, while fat can make stool hard and difficult to pass.
